About Us?

Welcome to AdlerAuge's coffee shop, where passion meets perfection in every cup. Our coffee shop is more than just a place to savor exceptional coffee, it's a cozy haven for coffee enthusiasts and those seeking a delightful escape. Our journey began with a commitment to sourcing the finest coffee beans, crafting exquisite blends, and creating a warm ambiance for our community. With each sip, we invite you to experience the artistry and dedication that go into making every cup memorable. Join us on a journey of flavors, aromas, and shared moments at AdlerAuge's!

Process & Products

Our coffee beans are selected carefully to ensure the quality of the products bring to the customers. After collecting coffee berries, they will be preprocessed and cleaned in order to remove impurities, create a premise to roast perfectly. The coffee mixing process was created to bring more diverse and richer coffee products, mixing in different proportions will produce different flavors. Last but not least, grinding coffee beans into powder, packaging and delivering to customers. Lots of premium coffee products are waiting to be picked up!!!

Tradition Of AdlerAuge's

AdlerAuge's is looking forward to renew day by day, bring customers to the diversity of the coffee world, help customers to have interesting experiences on different types of coffee. Thus, everyday, there will be an unique coffee product established by our shop, called "Cup of the day", when a customer had bought over 10 traditional cups, he or she will receive a voucher for the next order, accompanied by a chance to enter a spinning game and achieve awesome gifts.
